Four Key Exercises To Burn Fat Fast!

Reverse lunge with reach back

With your arms at your sides, hold yourself upright. Your body should be lowered till your left knee is at least 90 degrees bent as you lunge back with your right leg.Reach back over your shoulders and to the left as you lunge. At order to feel a stretch in the front of your thigh, push your right hip forward. Reverse the motion, going back to the beginning.Repetition is key. Take a step back with your left leg and stretch to the right and over your shoulders. Reach across the shoulder on the same side as your lead leg at all times. For 60 seconds, alternate the leg that is lunging.

Body-weight split squat jump

With your feet two to three feet apart and your right foot in front of your left, adopt a staggered posture. Legs bent, lower body in a lunge while keeping your torso erect. Jump now with sufficient force to lift both feet off the ground.Scissor kick your legs while in the air to land with your left leg in front. Repeat for 40 seconds then switching legs.

Turkish Getups

On your back, you will lie. The same knee should be bent, and one arm should be raised straight. Your other arm should be on the ground at your side. Raise your hips while rolling onto the arm that is supporting you. To stand up, swivel your extended knee under your bent knee. Reverse the exercise to go back to where you were before.

Squat-To-Press

Place your feet shoulder-width apart as you stand. Slowly bend your knees outward while keeping the weight just in front of your mid-section, keeping your hips slightly above your knees.
