Protein Powder

All nine necessary amino acids, including a significant amount of branched-chain amino acids, are present in whey protein isolates (WPI) (BCAAs). BCAAs are crucial for maintaining muscle glycogen and promoting muscular growth. Additionally, glutathione, a muscle-preserving antioxidant that is supported by whey, may lessen muscle loss during exercise. After working out, consume 20 to 40 grams of whey protein with creatine coupled with a high-glycemic carbohydrate like bread or potatoes to speed up blood glucose levels and boost the impact of your workout on muscle growth.

We advise taking 5 grams of creatine each day with your whey and carbohydrate source (about a heaping teaspoon or a serving of most creatine products). On days when you lift, it's best to do it right after your workout or around the same time on days off.
